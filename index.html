Element Categories

Category Description
Alkali Metals Highly reactive metals in Group 1 of the periodic table.
Alkaline Earth Metals Reactive metals in Group 2 of the periodic table.
Transition Metals Metals with variable oxidation states, found in the center of the periodic table.
Post Transition Metals Metals with lower melting points and softer than transition metals.
Metalloids Elements with properties of both metals and nonmetals.
Nonmetals Elements that are poor conductors of heat and electricity.
Halogens Highly reactive nonmetals in Group 17 of the periodic table.
Noble Gases Inert gases in Group 18 of the periodic table.
Lanthanides Rare earth metals, part of the f-block elements.
Actinides Radioactive elements, part of the f-block elements.
